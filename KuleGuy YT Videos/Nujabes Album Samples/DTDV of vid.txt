* Samples to Nujabes's Songs (6 Albums; My Favorites)

# Nujabes is favorite lofi hiphop, jazz rap, artists and the way he samples other songs and introduces lyrics into it is quite interesting, hence why I am making this video. I first found out about Nujabes because of another YouTuber (Glink), and I'm been enjoying his music ever since. I found the samples from whosampled.com and a google sheet. Sorry if there is any mistake in this video, I'm not perfect. I picked 6 albums because of familiarity and that they are my favorite albums.
