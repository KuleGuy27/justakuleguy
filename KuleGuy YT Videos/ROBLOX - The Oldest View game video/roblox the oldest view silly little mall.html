Exploring the Valley View Center Mall in ROBLOX - The Oldest View [silly little mall]

Kane Pixels, the man behind the backrooms, made another horrifying series called 'The Oldest View' and my boi vojnik_23 has recreated the mall in ROBLOX for us to play. Even though it's unfinished, it's a fairly accurate recreation and it gave me some more insight into what the mall was like. I filmed this video on my $20 HDMI capture card at 720p 60fps because that's the only option that supported 60fps. Rendered at 1080p because YouTube tends to compress videos.
