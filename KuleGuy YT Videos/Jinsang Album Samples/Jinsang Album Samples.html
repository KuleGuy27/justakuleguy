Holy cow, this took a long time to edit. Jinsang is one of my favorite lofi hip-hop artists and his samples are equally as interesting, hence why I am making this video. I found most of the samples on whosampled.com, no joke, and I compiled it all here. I don't own any images or songs here, all credit goes to the original creators, this video is NOT monetized (I don't make any money from YouTube anyways).
