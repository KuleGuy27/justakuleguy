Toyland Tussle! - An Actually Good Cuphead Clone??? (Review)

17 Sept 2025

Cuphead is one of my most favorite video games I've ever played and throughout the years, there have been clones or copies of it, and those clones quite frankly suck. A few weeks ago, Shaconda Cartoons (a channel I'm subscribed to), made a video on Toyland Tussle and that's how I found out about the game. It's essentially another Cuphead clone with the same artstyle and I'm impressed at how good it is to the other clones and that 1 person made the entire game by themselves.
Here is my review on "Toyland Tussle" and I hope you enjoy the video!
