* How I identify songs in background of videos

# Sometimes when watching content on the internet, I hear a song that I like and pull up Shazam or any song identifier, but it doesn't work because the YouTuber (or whoever) is talking while the background music is playing. Because of that, I have found a way to find a background music even when someone is talking. Mind you, this isn't foolproof, but it works.

Steps:
- Record or download video
- Convert video to mp3 (mp4/mkv/any video → mp3) using https://cloudconvert.com/
- Isolate music from vocals using https://vocalremover.org/
- Identify song using https://songfinder.gg/
