1 June 2025

I am finally done archiving most of my physical media collection! It took me about a year but I finally did it! Created 526 folders, crazy.

Referring to this video I made here: https://youtu.be/cmAttaNVxCc?si=FYst30lqGqqaWH0u

Some things I forgot to mention:

0:52 - I found another cool app on Windows called "Scanner" by Simon Knuth. It allows you to: scan multiple images, crop the images directly in the app, rotate or auto rotate, rename the file, delete it, save it as png, change the color mode and resolution, and etc... It's way more feature packed than the original "Windows Scan" app and I wish I knew about it sooner and talked about it in the video.

2:46 - When ripping DVDs, in Handbrake: go to Dimensions and check 'cropping' as none or custom and set all the values to 0. Cropping messes up the DVDs at times. There are parts of my DVDs where the videos aspect ratio changes from scene to scene, so leave the cropping feature unchecked.
