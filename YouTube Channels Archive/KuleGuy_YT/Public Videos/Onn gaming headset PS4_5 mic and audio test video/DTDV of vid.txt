* Onn Gaming Headset from Walmart Mic & Audio Test Review

# This is a review of the Onn gaming headset that I bought from Walmart for $25. It's compatible with PS4/PS5, has a 3.5mm cable (no splitter), a flip-to-mute mic on the left side, and a volume rocker. So far for me, it's doing pretty good, but it does feel cheap and there is this weird hickup noise that appears when I'm not listening to anything. Since I have this headset now, I don't have to take out my mic everytime so I guess that's a bonus. It's mid but fine.
