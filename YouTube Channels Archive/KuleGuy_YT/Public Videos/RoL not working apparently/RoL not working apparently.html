ROBLOX Apparently Doesn't Work on Linux Anymore...

29 February 2024

Hello guys, Happy Leap Day, the rarest day on Earth.

I play ROBLOX on Linux with Grapejuice, Vinegar, or Corky, and it's almost always working. But today, the ROBLOX developers who make ROBLOX work on Linux cut of the support because of some bad actors. It doesn't make sense(cents) to me at the moment, and I hope ROBLOX on Linux will be added back.
